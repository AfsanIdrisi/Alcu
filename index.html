Introducing Alcu India

In the heart of our brand name, Al stands tall for aluminum, while Cu proudly represents copper, elements drawn from the periodic table's wealth.

But Alcu India is more than just metal; it's a tale of hardworking artisans hailing from Moradabad, a small town in Uttar Pradesh, India. Moradabad, the epicenter of metal handicrafts made from precious metals like aluminum, copper, and brass, has transformed into a hub, exporting these crafts to destinations across the globe. Alcu emerges from this rich heritage, committed to offering Indian consumers a seamless journey into luxury, crafting products from the most precious materials.
